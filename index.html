The research

Hard Water Leaves A Measurable Film

The International Journal of Trichology has published research comparing hard water exposure and hair strength. Dermatology research has also examined how hard water can leave more soap residue on skin.

Ion exchange is exactly the technology inside AG Water Softener — the same mechanism, at shower scale.
